Adv. Priya Nair specialises in family law and succession planning, assisting clients with matrimonial matters, inheritance, and documentation for estate planning.

She is known for a thoughtful, discreet approach to sensitive family questions, helping clients understand legal options without unnecessary complexity.

Her practice includes wills, partition matters, and advisory work for families managing jointly held property.
