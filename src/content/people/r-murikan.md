Adv. R. Murikan is a Senior Partner at Murikan & Mangot, with extensive experience practising before the High Court of Kerala in property and civil litigation matters.

His practice focuses on title disputes, land acquisition issues, and complex civil appeals. Clients value his thorough preparation, familiarity with Kerala's land records systems, and measured approach to dispute resolution.

He has appeared in matters concerning property transactions, partition suits, and commercial disputes across Ernakulam and neighbouring districts.
