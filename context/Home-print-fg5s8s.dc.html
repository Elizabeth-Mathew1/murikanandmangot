About the Firm
A practice built on understanding
Murikan & Mangot is a law firm based in Ernakulam, Kochi, practising before the High Court of Kerala. We bring a measured, personal approach to legal counsel — listening carefully, advising clearly, and working with discretion at every stage.
Our practice spans property, corporate, family, and litigation matters. Whether you are an individual navigating a personal legal question or a business managing regulatory complexity, we are here to help you find clarity.

Our Approach
How we work
High Court Practice
Our advocates regularly appear before the High Court of Kerala, handling civil appeals, writ petitions, and commercial matters at the highest level in the state.
Local Knowledge
Rooted in Ernakulam's legal community, we bring deep familiarity with Kerala's regulatory landscape, land records, and local administrative processes.
Personal Counsel
Every matter receives direct attention from experienced advocates. We communicate clearly, set realistic expectations, and keep you informed at each step.
